About GestFlow

The GestFlow is an innovative technology that allows you to control media playback and navigate PDF documents using hand gestures. It utilizes computer vision and machine learning techniques to recognize specific hand gestures and translate them into corresponding actions.

With this system, you can control media playback features such as play, pause, volume control, and seek forward or backward, all with simple hand movements. You can also navigate through PDF documents by swiping left or right, zooming in or out, and scrolling up or down, providing a hands-free and intuitive experience.

The GestFlow offers a more immersive and interactive way of interacting with media and PDFs, making it ideal for various applications such as presentations, entertainment systems, and educational environments.

How to Use

Using the GestFlow is easy and straightforward. Follow these simple steps to start controlling media and PDFs with your hand gestures:

  1. Ensure your device's camera is properly set up and positioned to capture your hand movements.
  2. Open the GestFlow application or website on your device.
  3. Calibrate the system by following the on-screen instructions to align your hand within the frame.
  4. Once calibrated, you can now perform various hand gestures to control media and PDFs.
  5. Refer to the "Hand Signs" section for a list of supported gestures and their corresponding actions.
  6. Enjoy the hands-free and intuitive control experience!

Hand Signs

The GestFlow recognizes specific hand signs to perform different actions. Here are the supported hand signs and their corresponding actions:

  • Open Palm: Play or pause media playback
  • Closed Fist: Stop media playback
  • Swipe Left: Seek backward in media
  • Swipe Right: Seek forward in media
  • Swipe Up: Zoom in or scroll up in PDF
  • Swipe Down: Zoom out or scroll down in PDF

Perform these hand signs in front of the camera within the system's detection range to trigger the corresponding actions.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the GestFlow:

Can I use the system with any camera?

The GestFlow is compatible with most standard cameras and webcams. However, for optimal performance, we recommend using a camera with good resolution and frame rate.

Is the system available for different operating systems?

Yes, the GestFlow can be developed to work on various operating systems, including Windows, macOS, and Linux.

Does the system require any additional hardware?

No, the GestFlow utilizes computer vision techniques and requires only a camera to capture hand movements. There is no need for additional hardware.
